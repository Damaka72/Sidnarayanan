Rail Nationalisation

South Western Railways & West Midlands Railway

Challenge:

First major rail nationalisation in decades. Department for Transport mandated February 1st deadline (zero flexibility). 50+ business-critical IT contracts requiring commercial transition. 9 stakeholder groups with competing priorities. Passenger services cannot fail. No existing playbook - precedent-setting work.

Approach:

Developed commercial transition framework from first principles. Designed "single source of truth" contract database methodology. Created DfT statutory submission format. Built multi-stakeholder governance coordinating Programme, IT, Legal, Finance, DfT, SWR/WMR leadership, suppliers, and passengers. Operated under political and media scrutiny whilst managing contractual complexity.

Outcome:
¬£80M+ IT contract portfolio transitioned on deadline with zero operational disruption. Database methodology now WMR organisational standard. DfT submission format created industry template for future rail nationalisations. Commercial risk mitigation approach used across WMR. Framework will be referenced for all future rail transitions.
